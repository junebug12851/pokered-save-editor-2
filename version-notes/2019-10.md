### 2019-10-24 -- Restart using QML/QtQuick/JS instead of pure C++
<!-- commit: 7c59d670e1537577317b04e5cfd9a62f86fee5b0 -->
`7c59d67` - Twilight 

A major reset: the approach shifts from pure C++ to QML/QtQuick with JavaScript,
to make porting easier and the code (especially the databases) cleaner. The old
C++ `model/`/`store/` classes are removed, the data and assets are reorganized
under `assets/`, lodash is added, and the JS structure begins. The largest single
commit in the history by line count.
